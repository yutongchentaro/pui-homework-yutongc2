How to Use the Book of Answers

1. Clean up your mind when you are on the homepage.

2. Take 10 or 15 seconds to concentrate on your question.

3. Questions should be phrased in the style as: "should I travel this weekend?" or "is the choice the right one?"

4. When you sense the time is right, click on the "Find Your Answer" button.

5. Repeat the process for other questions you have, or share your results with others.
